Yale Rugby UK and Ireland Tour Fundraiser

This page is to help me fundraise for the Yale Men's Rugby tour to Ireland and the U.K. in March of 2020. This is a two-week-long tour where we will train alongside international teams, build relationships with rugby clubs in universities such as Trinity College Dublin, Oxford, and Cambridge, and fully immerse ourselves in Irish and British culture. Although we have received a number of generous donations from alumni, we are tasked with raising over $30,000 in order to fund the trip's expenses.My teammate Cavan Walsh and I have decided to up the ante with some personal challenges and rewards. On the first truly snowy,freezing day of winter, Cavan and I will toss on our swim trunks and for each $100 you all donate we will take one lap around Old Campus and do a ten second snow angel. If we reach our overall goal of $1,000 we will grab a boombox and follow up our laps with a Zumba/Dance class in the quad for all the people of Yale to enjoy.

If you feel so inclined, please donate below. Thank you for your generosity,
Nick Weinberger
